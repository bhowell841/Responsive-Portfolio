About Me

Raised by wolves in the Saskatchewan wilderness of Canada. Once found and rescued by humans he was given the family name Howell, because it is a play on howl. Wolves. Get it. Hilarious. Early life didn't provide many options for Brendan to develop adequate social skills. Thus you have the socially inept man in front of you today. The wilderness did provide Brendan the ability to develop and hone many survival skills including but not limited to making a mean cup of coffee and grilling a fantastic hamburger. Growing up in a pack of wolves Brendan had to be quick, but unfortunately he is not. Which explains how he was eventually captured by humans. He did develop the ability to run for long distances.
